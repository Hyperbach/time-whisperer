Welcome to Worklog

This installer sets up Worklog on your Mac and starts it automatically — no dragging, no extra steps.

  • Installs the Worklog app into your Applications folder.
  • Starts a small background helper that watches for Upwork screenshots.
  • The helper runs quietly and relaunches at login — no window, nothing in the Dock.

You can open Worklog any time to see status, pause, or uninstall.
